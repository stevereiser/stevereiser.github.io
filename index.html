Visit the Area

Longwood Garden

Experience the world of Longwood Gardens…a place to see dazzling displays that elevate the art of horticulture. Longwood is 30 minute drive from the hotel and is worth a visit if you have the time.

Go to Longwood's website

The Creamery

An outdoor beer garden, that is family friendly is a great time for any summer night. The Creamery is 25 minutes from the hotel and only 8 from Longwood

Go to Creamery's website

Christiana Mall

Tax free shopping! Have some time to kill? Head to the Christiana Mall and enjoy some tax free shopping. You can get down to the mall in about 6 minutes from the hotel.

Go to the mall's website

Philadelphia

Staying for the weekend, then take a drive into Philadelphia and spend the day walking around, checking out one of the many museums, run up the stairs like Rocky, eat some cheesesteaks, and see the Liberty Bell. Philly is only a 45 minute drive away.
